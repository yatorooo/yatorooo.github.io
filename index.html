I am Teke Xu, based in London. I completed my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at the University of Groningen, where my thesis studied the water hammer problem and its numerical analysis. I later worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University College London on thermo-fluid modelling, uncertainty analysis, and optimisation for CO2 transport systems. My work sits at the intersection of applied mathematics, system modelling, simulation, and computational analysis for complex engineering problems.
